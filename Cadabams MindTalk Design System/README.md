# Cadabams MindTalk — Design System

> Clinical-grade mental health super-app from Cadabams Group + Mindtalk. Real-clinician therapy + structured 30/45/90-day journeys + an AI companion ("Doctor Rhea") + self-care toolkit (assessments, journaling, breathwork, trackers) — all in one continuous experience.
>
> **Thesis:** own the gap *between* therapy sessions, where recovery actually happens or stalls.

The product has to feel **professional enough to trust with sensitive data, and warm enough to never feel clinically cold.** Think golden-hour, not corporate-medical.

---

## Audience

Mobile-first, primarily Indian users.

- **Patients** — people managing or recovering from mental-health conditions, or growing emotionally. People sometimes in genuine distress: design must never shame or alarm.
- **Clinicians** — psychologists, psychiatrists, clinical psychologists, behavioural therapists. Need structured, accurate clinical tooling.

## Content fundamentals

The voice is **warm, plain-language, second person.** Every surface that talks to the user reads like a calm friend who happens to be clinically informed.

### Tone & casing
- **Sentence case** for all UI. Headlines and CTAs are sentence case (`Start your journey`, `Book Now`, `Subscribe to Journal`) — never Title Case Like This.
- **Overlines / labels** are short, all-caps, tracked-out: `TODAY'S PROMPT`, `MINDFUL SPACE`, `DAILY PROMPT`. Use sparingly to anchor a section.
- **Numbers** get *display-size* emphasis (assessment percentages, day counts, XP, streak counts). Big number, small label below.

### Pronouns
- **Second person.** "Your safe space for thoughts and feelings", "How are you feeling today?", "What you'll achieve".
- **First-person possessives** *only* on user-owned objects: "Your Journey", "Your Thoughts", "Your Routine".
- Greetings name the user: `Good Morning, Sarah` · `Hi Sarah, how are you feeling?`

### Concrete over poetic
Always prefer the specific over the aspirational.

✅ `Day 9 of 90 · Anxiety Relief`
❌ `Your wellness journey awaits`

✅ `What are three small things that made you smile today?`
❌ `Pause and reflect on your inner light`

### Never shame, always soft-land
This is a clinical product used by people in distress. Gamification mechanics (streaks, XP, gems) **must never punish a miss.**

- Streak missed → "Freeze your streak" / "Revive" — never "You broke your streak."
- No streak yet → "Complete your first reflection today to start your streak!"
- Skipped session → reschedule offered first; cancel is secondary.
- Crisis-relevant copy is calm, never alarmist. The Quick Relief / Emergency Reset surface uses the same warm orange, not red.

### AI safety & disclosure
Wherever AI generates content (assessment summaries, journal reflections, recommendations):

1. Show the **AI-generated pill** — small peach/cream pill, sparkle icon, label `AI-generated` or `AI-generated summary`.
2. Include explicit non-diagnosis copy:
   > *"This summary is generated by AI based on your answers. It is **not a diagnosis** and should be used as a reflection aid, not as medical advice."*
3. Where AI replies in conversation (Doctor Rhea), the same pill anchors the reply.

### Vocabulary
Mental health terminology is used **accurately and respectfully** — `Generalized Anxiety Disorder`, `M.Phil`, `PhD`, `CBT`, `PHQ-9`, `GAD-7`. Never softened into vagueness, but always paired with plain-language explanation.

### Emoji
- **Used sparingly and only with intent.** The mood reflection screen uses 5 emoji faces (😣 😔 😀 😊 😄) as feeling-state inputs — that's the dominant use.
- **Not used as icons** anywhere else. Iconography is line-icons (see below).
- **Not used in body copy.** Headings and product strings are emoji-free.

### Examples to copy-paste from
- `Your safe space for thoughts and feelings.`
- `Free Flow — Write whatever is on your mind. No prompts, just you.`
- `Not sure who to choose? Answer a few quick questions and we'll match you with the right specialist.`
- `Mood check-in completed`
- `Your activity assessment results — Overall balance: Moderate (72%)`
- `Curated by Dr. Ananya, Clinical Psychologist`

## Visual foundations

### Color
**Three layers, in this order:**

1. **Cream canvas** (`#FAF7F4`) — the breathing room. Almost every screen sits on this. Pure white is reserved for *floating* card surfaces, never the page background.
2. **Warm orange CTA** (`#F97316`) — the only loud color. Reserved for primary actions, active states, current-day pills, and the brand-mark.
3. **Coral → orange gradient** for hero blocks (login Continue, Free Flow journal hero, home greeting). Always horizontal, coral on the left, orange on the right.

Categorical content (Therapy, Journal, Breathe, Assess) gets **soft pastel tints** — pale blue, pink, green, peach — *behind* line-icon glyphs. The glyph carries the color; the tint is the wash. Sleep & evening content uses soft purple. Dark hero cards (`Talk to Dr. AI`, `Upcoming Session`) use near-black ink (`#1C2433`) with white text.

See `colors_and_type.css` for the full token set.

### Type
- **Family:** Inter (variable, optical-size axis active). Bundled locally — see `fonts/`.
- **High weight contrast:** body is 400, section headings jump to 700/800. No 500/600 in copy unless it's a button label.
- **Tight tracking on display sizes** (`-0.02em`), neutral on body.
- **Numbers get display emphasis** — `tnum` enabled — for stats, day counts, percentages.
- **Date chips** are squircles with the weekday two-letter label above the date number; the current day is filled solid orange.
- **Overlines** (`TODAY'S PROMPT`, `MINDFUL SPACE`) are 11–12px, tracked +0.08em, bold, brand-orange.

> **Font:** Inter Variable (regular + italic) is bundled in `fonts/` and loaded via `@font-face` in `colors_and_type.css`. The optical-size axis (`opsz`) is set to 16 by default; bump to 24–32 on display sizes for tighter spacing. No external network fetch.firm or attach the actual font files** — drop into `fonts/` and update the `@import` in `colors_and_type.css`.

### Backgrounds & imagery
**Three illustration registers — pick the right one for the surface:**

| Register | Where it's used | Look |
|---|---|---|
| **Photography** (golden-hour, nature) | Guided audio / visualization tiles — Forest Bathing, Ocean Soundscape, Morning Clarity, Sleep Stories. | Always photographic. Never illustrated. Calm, warm, golden-hour palette. |
| **Painterly landscapes** | Journey hero covers — 90 Day Emotional Reset, Deep Sleep, etc. | Soft brushstrokes, abstract. Orange-toned dunes, mountain ridges, sunset hills. |
| **Soft fabric / silk-flow** | Onboarding heroes (Phone Login). | Translucent silk wisps in coral and orange. Ethereal, low-pressure. |

Two recurring **motifs**:
- **Sprout / plant** — growth metaphor. Used on Journal landing (a seedling growing from orange dunes) — replaces aggressive streak graphics.
- **Doctor Rhea mascot** — a friendly cute robot. Used wherever the AI surfaces (Talk to Dr. AI card, AI chat).

Photography has a **warm cast** universally. Cool / blue images appear only in the audio thumbnails (ocean, water) and only when the topic demands it. No black-and-white, no heavy grain, no high-contrast cinema.

### Cards & surfaces
- **Standard card:** white, `border-radius: 18–24px`, soft shadow `0 8px 24px rgba(15,23,42,.08)`, no border. Floats on cream.
- **Hero card:** larger radius `28–40px`, full-bleed background (gradient or photo), white text overlaid bottom-aligned. May add a soft outer glow when interactive.
- **Locked / disabled card:** flat fog `#F4F2EE`, no shadow, lock icon glyph centered.

### Borders & lines
- **No borders** on cards by default — separation is via shadow on cream.
- **Hairlines** (`#ECE6DE`) inside cards to separate rows (e.g. session details).
- **Selection rings** are `2px solid var(--mt-orange-500)` on white surfaces; `1.5px` on cream.

### Corner radii
Heavy and consistent.

| Use | Radius |
|---|---|
| Chips, pills, CTA buttons | fully rounded (`999px`) |
| Inputs, small icon-tile | `14px` |
| Standard card | `18–24px` |
| Hero card | `28–40px` |

### Shadows
Three soft elevations — never hard, never colored except for orange glows on primary CTAs.
- `sh-1` ambient (cards on cream)
- `sh-2` standard card
- `sh-3` hero / lifted
- `sh-glow-orange` on the primary CTA fab and the active journey-step circle.

### Buttons & states
- **Primary** — orange fill (or coral→orange gradient on heroes), white label, fully rounded, `padding: 16px 24px`. Soft orange glow.
- **Secondary** — white fill, ink text, fully rounded, hairline border on cream.
- **Tertiary / chip** — fully rounded pill, ink text on white, brand-orange when active. Active = filled black for filter chips, filled orange for CTAs.
- **Hover** — subtle darken (~6%) on the orange; on white pills, background darkens to `#FBF5EF`.
- **Press** — *shrink to 0.98* with `transform: scale()` and a slight inset shadow. No color flash.
- **Disabled** — `opacity: 0.5`, no shadow.
- **Active tab / current day** — filled orange circle with white glyph/text.

### Motion
- **Easing:** smooth out (`cubic-bezier(.22,.61,.36,1)`) for entrances and presses; in-out for crossfades.
- **Duration:** 140ms for press feedback, 220ms for transitions, 380ms for hero reveals.
- **No bounce.** Spring-y motion contradicts the calm tone.
- **Crossfades over slides** for content swaps. Modal sheets rise from below.
- **Breath / pulse animations** on the audio play button and the breathing-exercise circle — slow (4–7s loop), purely visual.

### Transparency & blur
- **Top bars** sometimes use `backdrop-filter: blur(12px)` over the cream — subtle, only when content scrolls behind.
- **Overlay sheets** (option pickers, country selectors) dim the underlying screen with `rgba(15,23,42,0.45)`.
- The silk-flow login illustration uses *layered translucency* in the asset itself, not via CSS.

### Layout rules
- **Mobile screen gutter:** `20px` left/right.
- **Top bar:** profile avatar (left), screen title (center or left), one contextual action (right) — search, share, settings, more.
- **Bottom tab bar:** 5 tabs — `Home · Explore · AI · Appts · Profile`. The center "AI" tab is a raised orange floating circle with the sparkle mark; the others are line icons. Active tab = orange + filled icon.
- **Vertical rhythm:** consistent `24px` between sections, `12–16px` between rows in a list.
- **Numbers and metrics anchor** the eye — keep large, ink-900, with the label as small ink-500 below.

### Sensitive-design rules (codified)

These are **non-negotiable** rules, not preferences:

1. **Quick Relief / Emergency Reset is always reachable.** Surface it in the home grid and the Explore "Quick Relief" rail. Calm copy, never alarmist, never red.
2. **Soft-land all gamification.** Streaks, XP, Gems, Stars: a miss is "freeze" or "revive." A zero-state is "Complete your first ___ to start."
3. **Privacy is first-class.** The Assessment Intro card explicitly states `Private & Confidential — Your results are only visible to you.` The Session Details surface has a separate `Share Journal — select entries to share with Dr. Reddy` action — sharing is *opt-in per entry,* never default.
4. **AI disclosure is mandatory.** Pill + non-diagnosis copy on every AI-generated surface.
5. **Crisis-grade contrast.** Body text on cream is `#0E1726` (16.8:1) — not the trendy gray-on-cream that fails WCAG.

## Iconography

### System
The app uses **single-weight line icons** with rounded caps and joins, ~1.5–2px stroke at 24×24. Style is closest to **Lucide / Feather** — geometric, friendly, no decorative flourish. Filled variants exist only for *active states* in the bottom tab bar and for category glyphs sitting inside soft tinted tiles (sun, moon, heart, lightning).

We did not have access to the production icon font/sprite. **We default to Lucide via CDN** as the closest visual match. When the actual icon set is available, drop the SVGs into `assets/icons/` and switch the references.

> **🚩 Icon substitution flag:** Lucide is a *substitution.* If the production app uses a custom set or a different library (Phosphor, Tabler, Iconoir all match the visual register), please attach.

### Specific iconography rules
- **Glyphs inside category tiles** (Therapy / Journal / Breathe / Assess on home; Gratitude / Sleep on journal): always sit inside a 14-px-radius tinted square, glyph centered, glyph color matches a deeper shade of the tint.
- **Brand sparkle** (4-pointed star with cross-glow) — used on the AI tab, AI-generated pill, and Doctor Rhea avatar. We use a custom SVG since this is a brand mark, not a generic icon.
- **Mood faces** are emoji (😣 😔 😀 😊 😄) — not icons. The selected one sits on an orange filled tile.
- **No emoji elsewhere.**

## Information architecture (cheat sheet)

**Five-tab bottom nav:** `Home · Explore · AI · Appts · Profile`. Active = orange + filled icon. The AI tab is a raised orange circle.

**Top bar pattern:** profile avatar (left) · screen title (center or left) · contextual action (right — search, share, settings).

**Tools that can be self-selected or clinician-prescribed always have two tabs:** `Explore` and `Assigned`.

**Repeating UI patterns** (all live in the UI kit):
- Hero card with full-bleed gradient or photo
- Mood color dots row (5–6 emotional colors)
- AI-generated pill with sparkle
- Photo content tiles (~1.4:1) with title overlay at bottom
- Therapist trust card (circular photo, name, credentials, specialty chips, ★ rating, fee, Book Now)
- Daily Growth timeline (horizontal Mon–Sun strip + chronological feed)
- Therapist matching CTA hero
- Quick Relief surface
